This is the update record of data revision made after the release in December 2009. We sincerely apologize for the inconvenience caused. Data for all items shown below have been updated in the latest version.

October 5, 2016
vol.1 Engine - Assembly Instructions　Fixed the display of the next parts number. Page6 : A-85 → A-86, Page12 : Engine1 → Engine2.
March 10, 2015
There was a mistake to the red circle indicating the adhesion position of C7 and C8 in parts sheet of the Vol.2 frame. We apologize for any inconvenience and confusion this may have caused.
January 31, 2011
On the N-1, N-2, N-3, and N-4 parts data sheets for the Vol. 4 exhaust, the colors of the sheets have been adjusted. The PDF data for the parts data sheets (colored version) have been updated accordingly.
December 13, 2010
The C39 and C43 parts in the part sheets of Vol. 2 frame were numbered the other way round.
We apologize for any inconvenience and confusion this may have caused.
April 5, 2010

Following the release in December 2009, it was found that some parts could not be assembled as shown in the pictures. Accordingly, the parts sheets shown below were revised. The revision date is shown on the page of revised parts sheets. We sincerely apologize for the inconvenience caused.

  • Colored version : vol.2 Frame, vol.3 Steerin, vol.4 Exhaust Pipes
  • White version : vol.2 Fram, vol.3 Steering, vol.4 Exhaust Pipes, vol.5 Tire
