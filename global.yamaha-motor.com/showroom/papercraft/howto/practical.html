How to make the Fuel Tank

Although comprised of only a few parts, the Fuel Tank has complex curves. However, it can be assembled beautifully by following the instruction booklet and the numbers printed on the parts. The key is in creating realistic curves. Curving of each part and angles of glued parts are important.

Movie in step

How to make the Front Tire

The tire is the most difficult part. Detailed work is necessary in various parts, including where tiny glue flaps are lined up like saw teeth, but if you work carefully it should come out fine. Acquire efficient work skills from the video clip, and assemble with care, with precision in each part and detailed adjustment of parts positioning.

How to make the Steering

The key to making the Steering which consists of many thin tube-shaped parts is to create smooth curves. Make beautiful tubes using your palm, aluminum rod, etc. Tweezers will be used frequently, since many of the parts are too small for fingers.
*Assembly procedure to create Steering in photo will be explained in “Final Assembly.”
